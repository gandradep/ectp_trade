Ecuador–Taiwan Trade Office connects companies, institutions, and investors from Ecuador and Taiwan to promote exports, imports, investment, innovation, and business cooperation, facilitating access to business opportunities in both markets.

About Us

Ecuador–Taiwan Trade Office is an international trade initiative dedicated to strengthening economic relations between Ecuador and Taiwan. Through trade promotion services, market intelligence, business matchmaking, and trade facilitation, we support companies and institutions in developing sustainable, long-term business opportunities.

Why Taiwan?

Taiwan is one of Asia’s most dynamic economies and a global leader in innovation, technology, and advanced manufacturing. Its market offers significant opportunities for high-quality agricultural products, sustainable solutions, and international business cooperation and investment projects.

Why Ecuador?

Ecuador offers a strategic location on the Pacific coast, a diverse export portfolio recognized worldwide, and attractive investment opportunities across productive, technological, and sustainable sectors. Its strong connectivity to international markets makes Ecuador a valuable strategic partner for business development throughout Latin America.

Strategic Partnerships

The Ecuador–Taiwan Trade Office promotes collaboration with chambers of commerce, trade promotion organizations, universities, business associations, public institutions, and private sector entities to strengthen economic cooperation and foster business opportunities between Ecuador and Taiwan.
